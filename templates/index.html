AAS WorldWide Telescope is a tool for showcasing astronomical data and knowledge. It’s not a physical telescope — it’s open-source software, data, and stories living on your devices and the Web.

WWT is brought to you by the non-profit American Astronomical Society (AAS), the major organization of professional astronomers in North America and the publisher of Sky & Telescope magazine. WWT’s hosting is supported by the .NET Foundation and the WWT project has been supported by the National Science Foundation, the Gordon and Betty Moore Foundation, and Microsoft.
